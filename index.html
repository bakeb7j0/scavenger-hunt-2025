Welcome to Lisa and Lisa on the Hunt 2025!

How to Play:

You have 7 stops to make on your adventure. For each step, you will receive and envelope with a QR code. Scanning the QR code will reveal a puzzle. Solve the word scramble to reveal the second clue. Using the answer to the word scramble, and the contents of the clue you revealed, you should be able to quickly figure out where to go. If you arrive at a business, head in, they are expecting you. If you end up at a landmark, seach for another envelope, it will have instructions indicating what must be done at the stop, which will include sending an SMS to the group chat with proof of task completion. Upon completing the stop, you will either be given a new envelope, or you will receive a new link in the group chat. Lather, Rinse, Repeat until 5pm!



Frequently Asked Questions

How do we get around?

All transportation will be provided for you!


How do I scan a QR code?

Open your camera app
point it at the QR code and hold the phone still
a link will appear on the screen, press it and it will take you to the appropriate page on the site


What if we are stumped?

At the bottom of each page are expandable sections with instructions on what to do with the puzzle or clue on the page,
as well as one to call for help via either text or phone call. Don't be shy!
Don't spend a lot of time on something you can't figure out!
