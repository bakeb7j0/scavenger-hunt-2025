The Proposal

Activity Instructions

How to Complete This Stop:

1. Find a stranger willing to record a short video on your phone

2. Taking the provided jewrely box, one of you must get down on one knee (slowly, your goal is to attract attention)

3. Look longingly at the other, and say "Lisa, would you ... do me the great honor ... of watering my plants while I am out of town?". Feel free to embelish your proposal as you feel moved.

4. SMS the video to the group chat, and you will receive your next clue via a link in the chat.

Ways to get help:

  • Send an SMS SOS
  • Give the gamemaster a ring.
